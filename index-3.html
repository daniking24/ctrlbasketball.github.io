About the Coach

Danielle King

Danielle King is a former Division I point guard for the Marquette Golden Eagles women's basketball team. A Chicago native and Bloom Township High School standout who averaged 28.4 PPG and 6.3 APG as a senior.

She started at Marquette over 128 career games, winning three Big East Championships and scoring 1,300+ career points. She has coached at the varsity level and served on staff at Texas Southern University before founding CTRL Basketball.

The Training

Spacing, court vision, passing accuracy, and competitive reps that sharpen IQ under pressure.

Faithful Hoops Initiative

The Hall of Fame Life

A faith-based mentorship program built for college athletes navigating the pressures of student-athlete life. We believe every athlete can live a Hall of Fame life — one defined not by stats, but by values, purpose, and faith in Jesus Christ.

FHI guides athletes through the challenges and triumphs of collegiate sports, empowering them to grow spiritually, lead on their campuses, and mentor others along the way.

R
Resilience
Rising through adversity with your faith as the foundation. Every setback is a setup for something greater.
C
Confidence
Knowing your identity goes beyond your sport. Confidence rooted in purpose, not performance.
T
Togetherness
Community built on shared faith and shared goals. You don't have to navigate this journey alone.
